NIGHT

Lightweight spaceship

Night is a very first version spaceship that can travel around the galaxy. The main feature is its fast flight speed and flying through the wormhole in the Milky Way. During the wormhole trip, you can enjoy the unique cosmic scenery during the trip. There are many different cabins and services in the spacecraft, allowing travelers to enjoy the journey during the flight.

CABINS FACILITIES

Passenger cabin

There are 2 passenger cabins on the spaceship, each cabin contains 50 seats. The seats recline both for comfort – especially during launch and return. Each seat is equipped with a seatback screen, the displays will show live flight data including speed, g-force, and remaining boost time. The cabin also has windows, which allow passengers to get the best view during the trip.

Shower Cabin

There are 20 shower machines in the cabin, allowing passengers to clean their bodies. The shower machine is developed by high-tech, which is convenient for washing the body in the case of weightlessness. It has different modes for passengers to choose from. Passengers only need to stand inside the machine and select the desired mode. For example, body cleaning, head only, drying, etc.

Entertainment Cabin

The entertainment cabin has several areas for passengers to enjoy and spend time during the trip. It contains workout sections and game sections. The workout sections allow passengers to exercise to ensure that they maintain their best physical condition during long-term travel. The game sections have a VR area and a board game area, where passengers can play with their partners or new friends.
